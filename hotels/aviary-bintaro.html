Property Description & Site Context

Disclaimer: Prepared based on visual inspection and publicly available information; no management interviews or technical documentation review.

1. Property Description
  • Total Area: ± 0.40 Ha (estimated, subject to verification)
  • Building Area: ± 15,000 m² (multi-storey hotel building)
  • Rooms: 125 rooms & serviced apartment suites (1–3BR suites also offered).
  • Floors: 12 storeys

Aviary Hotel Bintaro is a four-star hotel located within the Bintaro area, South Tangerang, forming part of the Greater Jakarta metropolitan region. The property is situated in an urban township environment, surrounded by established residential, commercial, and retail developments.

The hotel was developed as a city hotel. It primarily serves business and leisure travellers. The property is characterised by a vertical building configuration typical of urban hotels in South Tangerang, with guest rooms and supporting facilities arranged across multiple floors.

The hotel provides standard facilities associated with a four-star city hotel classification, including guest rooms, food and beverage outlets, meeting facilities, and recreational amenities. Observations and descriptions are based on publicly available information and a limited physical inspection, without verification through stakeholder interviews or review of architectural, engineering, or operational documentation.

2. Location
2.1. Address

Aviary Hotel Bintaro is located at Jl. Boulevard Bintaro Blok B7 No. D3–3A, Pondok Jaya, Pondok Aren, South Tangerang, Banten 15224.

2.2. Frontage Road Condition

The road in front of the property is a two-way main road within the Bintaro area. Each direction has an approximate width of 8 metres and is separated by a central median with an estimated width of 2.5 metres, landscaped with grass and palm trees.

Each direction consists of three traffic lanes, providing good capacity for daily traffic movement. The road is also equipped with road shoulders that function as pedestrian walkways, supported by a closed drainage system.

The road surface is constructed with asphalt pavement, which is commonly used for primary urban roads in the area and appears suitable for regular vehicular use.

2.3. Access and connectivity

Jl. Boulevard Bintaro Jaya has an approximate total length of 6.6 kilometres based on Google Maps measurements. Aviary Hotel Bintaro is located along the middle section of this road, providing balanced access in both directions.

The property is located approximately 2.6 kilometres from the Jakarta–Serpong Toll Road and around 4.0 kilometres from the Jakarta–Cinere Toll Road, depending on the specific access ramps used. These toll roads provide connectivity to South Jakarta, Serpong, Depok, and the wider Greater Jakarta area.

The hotel enjoys strong regional and local accessibility due to its position along Jl. Boulevard Bintaro Jaya, one of the primary arterial roads within the Bintaro Jaya township.

Key Connectivity includes:
  • Direct access to Tol Jakarta–Serpong (JORR W2 Utara) via Pintu Tol Bintaro, providing efficient linkage to Jakarta Selatan and central Jakarta.
  • Connection to Tol Kunciran–Serpong (JORR 2) to the north, enabling access toward Bandara Soekarno-Hatta and the wider Greater Jakarta toll network.
  • Continuous internal road connections linking Sector 7 to surrounding residential, commercial, and mixed-use sectors within Bintaro Jaya.

3. Surrounding Location

The subject property is located within Bintaro Sector 7, which functions as the primary business and commercial centre of the Bintaro area. The surrounding land use is dominated by offices, service-oriented businesses, and supporting commercial facilities, with limited residential presence.

Sector 7 accommodates a concentration of time-intensive activities, including meetings, project-based work, and service appointments that frequently involve non-local visitors. These activities often extend across multiple days and are commonly distributed across several locations within the area, creating a practical need for nearby accommodation.

In addition to conventional hotel rooms, the subject property provides 1–3 bedroom serviced apartment units. This accommodation format is typically utilised by groups, project teams, families accompanying business travellers, and longer-stay occupants. It is also consistent with an environment where business, institutional, and service-related activities require extended presence rather than short, single-night stays.

The presence of restaurants, cafés, and daily service facilities within Sector 7 supports these longer and group-based stays by functioning as essential living infrastructure, enabling occupants to maintain normal daily routines outside the hotel.

Accordingly, the surrounding location contributes materially to the existence and suitability of hotel and serviced apartment accommodation within Sector 7, as the area structurally supports both short-term business stays and longer-duration, multi-occupant accommodation demand.
